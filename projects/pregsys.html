Pregsys: Infant Mortality Rate Analysis in India

  • Analyzed Infant Mortality Rate (IMR) in India using National Family Health Survey (2015–16) data.
  • Performed data cleaning and pre-processing using Pandas for accuracy and consistency.
  • Applied multiple regression to assess the impact of maternal care indicators on IMR.
  • Visualized trends and key insights using charts/graphs for clear communication of findings.
